Research

Ongoing & Completed Work

Applying machine learning and LLMs to financial prediction, corporate risk, and scientific domains.

FinTabLLM — M&A Withdrawal Prediction

Built a framework to serialize structured tabular M&A data to natural language and fine-tuned LLaMA-3 with parameter-efficient methods (PEFT/LoRA) on multi-GPU infrastructure to predict merger withdrawals.

+2% ROC AUC vs. tree-based baselines

Macroeconomic ML for Corporate Bond Risk

Modeled interactions between bond characteristics and macroeconomic indicators using MLPs and linear regression to predict corporate bond excess returns from a sparse curated feature set.

+3% out-of-sample R²
MLPLinear RegressionFeature EngineeringTime Series

The Voice of Risk

Developed verbal and acoustic ML features (nonanswers, disfluency) from CEO earnings calls using Wav2Vec2 and multinomial inverse regression to improve corporate misconduct prediction.

+2.5% accuracy in misconduct prediction
Wav2Vec2NLPSpeech ProcessingCausal Inference

EPS Prediction with Multimodal Data

Tokenized time-series and 10-Q reports; used RAG to extract forward-looking statements; predicted next-quarter EPS by fusing textual and numerical signals.

+5% R² over time-series baseline
RAGTransformersMultimodalTime SeriesNLP

Physics-Informed LLMs — RHIC Trigger Prediction

Converted collider simulation data into textual prompts; fine-tuned LLMs to classify high-energy physics trigger events at the Relativistic Heavy Ion Collider.

Complete
